FAQs

How do I set my side panel to be on the left side?
Follow the recommended settings instructions
  1. Go to Chrome Settings (⋮ menu > Settings)
  2. Click on "Appearance" in the sidebar
  3. Scroll down to "Side panel position"
  4. Select "Show on left"
How do I change shortcut to Cmd+S instead?
Follow the recommended settings instructions
  1. Go to Chrome Settings (⋮ menu > Settings)
  2. Click on "Extensions" in the sidebar
  3. Click on "Keyboard shortcuts" at the bottom
  4. Find "BarCat" and customize the shortcut
How can I change the default space when Chrome opens?

You can either follow the steps below or click here to open extension options directly.

  1. Go to Chrome Settings (⋮ menu > Settings)
  2. Click on "Extensions" in the sidebar
  3. Find "BarCat" and click "Details"
  4. Click "Extension options" to open the settings page
  5. Change the "Default Space Name" to your preferred space
Can I move tabs between spaces?

Yes! Right click on the tab and use the "Move to space" option.

Where can I see my stored pinned tabs in spaces?
Your pinned tabs are stored in your Chrome bookmarks under the "BarCat" folder. Each space has its own subfolder containing its pinned tabs for easy access and organization.
Are my spaces and pinned tabs synced across devices?
Yes! Since your spaces and pinned tabs are stored in Chrome bookmarks, they automatically sync across all your devices where you're signed into Chrome with the same account.
